Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from Tuesday to Sunday! You can choose your preferred delivery time slot (Morning, Afternoon, or Evening) during checkout.

How much is the delivery?

Delivery is $20 islandwide.

Are we Halal certified?

No, we are not Halal certified, but all our chickens are sourced from Halal slaughterhouses and slaughtered according to Islamic practices.

Are your products really fresh?

Absolutely. As our motto says: "Any fresher, it was running yesterday." We process and deliver rapidly to ensure peak freshness.

How do I pay?

When you checkout, your order details are securely sent to our WhatsApp. We will confirm your order and provide payment instructions (PayNow/PayLah!) there. Do note that orders will only be processed after payment is made.
